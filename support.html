Frequently Asked Questions

Are the results accurate?

No, Brain Joke Maker is strictly for entertainment purposes. The results are randomly generated based on the name input and date to provide fun and funny outcomes.

How does Pair Mode work?

Enter two names (yours and a partner/friend) to see compatibility. We highlight matching words in Gold!

App icons appear as 'X' or boxes?

This is usually a temporary loading issue after an update. Please try completely closing and restarting the app. If the issue persists, reinstalling the app will fix the missing icons.

The app crashed, what should I do?

Please try restarting the app. If the problem persists, reinstalling the app usually fixes most issues.
